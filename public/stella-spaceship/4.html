Part 4: Teamwork in Action

She called out, “Blink! We’ve got a mission—let’s fix that tube!”

Blink zipped out of his port with a cheerful beep and got to work. Stella carefully guided him using her robotic arm.

Together, they wrapped the magnetic tape tightly around the fuel tube, sealing the loose connection.

With a hum and a happy beep, everything clicked back into place.
