Part 1: The Dreamy Journey

In the quiet corner of the galaxy, there was a bright little starship named Stella.

Stella wasn’t just any spaceship—she could think, talk, and dream! Her favorite dream? To visit the shimmering rings of Saturn.

One day, her chance finally came. Stella zoomed past the Moon, zipped by Mars, and soared through the stars.
