Part 3: Stay Calm and Think

Panic bubbled up in Stella’s circuits, but then she remembered what her builder, Captain Orion, once told her:

“Every problem has a solution—you just have to stay calm and think it through.”

Stella slowed down and floated quietly. First, she listed what she had:

1. A robotic arm

2. A roll of magnetic tape

3. A tiny maintenance drone named Blink
